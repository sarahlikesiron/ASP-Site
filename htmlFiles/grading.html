Grading

I feel that my website follows the CRAP design techniques, there is a unified, clean design among all pages, and I think that the colors match and contrast pretty well. The text and imags that I have included on the pages flow well and are organized in a logical way, allowing people to see information on each page that they would want to and expect to see there. I feel like my use of a timeline on the history page makes it more interesting, with the inclusion of some javascript to make the dropdown in the one section functional. I used both HTML5 and CSS3 on my site, not relying on some of the older features, instead using the newer ones. I have included a references page, linking the sites I used to find the information, besides the infomation I already knew about the park. All of the images are my own.
